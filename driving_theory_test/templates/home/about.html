Theory Test Simulation

The developer was inspired to create this site after the traumatic experience of taking the real theory exam and failing twice.

All questions are randomly selected from a database of 150 test questions adapted from the Driving License Requisition Manual issued by the Thailand Department of Land Transport.

The style, timing and difficulty of the full simulation match those of the real exam, which means answers to incorrect questions are revealed only upon finishing the test. Navigating through questions using the browser's 'back' and 'refresh' button may lead to error messages. Wording of questions mimics the real exam. It will be occasionally confusing and often use non-native sounding English.
